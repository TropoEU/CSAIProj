Our Story

Bob's Pizza started in 1985 when Bob Martinez opened a small pizzeria in downtown with a simple dream: to bring authentic Italian flavors to our community.

Three decades later, we're still using the same family recipes passed down from Bob's grandmother in Naples. Every pizza is made with love, fresh ingredients, and a commitment to quality that has made us a local favorite.

Today, the Martinez family continues Bob's legacy, serving thousands of happy customers every week. From our kitchen to your table - that's the Bob's Pizza promise.
